Trash Reaper - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Introduction

Alexender ("we", "us", or "our") built the Trash Reaper app as a free, ad-supported game. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

Information Collection and Use

Trash Reaper does not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users. The game does not require account creation, login, or any form of registration.

Game progress data (such as level progress, in-game currency, and weapon upgrades) is stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob — We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data such as device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

This game is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The ads displayed are configured to comply with applicable regulations.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, there is no risk of your personal data being compromised through our services. All game data is stored locally on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
